Welcome to Bachoo Garage!
We are a TATA Motors Authorized Service Station located in Mumbai City and established by Mr. E W Carvalho in the year 1951.
Since then, with our 65+ years of experience in the field and our team of highly trained, certified and professional technicians, we have established ourselves as the foremost TATA Motors Service Station in the Western Region of India that is purely into Servicing of Commercial Vehicles.
We firmly believe in Quality of Service and deliver the highest level of consistent, reliable and honest service to your every visit.
We have branches and affiliates are located at Mumbai, Thane and Bhiwandi.
We maintain up-to-date equipment with advanced technology and the latest diagnostic systems to accurately diagnose and repair your vehicle. Our workshop is maintained and our services are conducted in a highly organized and clean atmoshpere, in order to ensure that your vehicle is serviced to the highest quality and with no delays (we strive to maintain same-day delivery).
